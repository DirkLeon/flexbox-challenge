Metroid is a renowned science fiction video game franchise created by Nintendo. The series follows the adventures of Samus Aran, a powerful bounty hunter equipped with advanced power armor, as she explores alien planets and battles hostile creatures known as Metroids. First introduced in 1986, Metroid is celebrated for its atmospheric exploration, non-linear gameplay, and emphasis on isolation. The franchise has evolved over the years, introducing innovative mechanics and storytelling elements. With its iconic protagonist, memorable alien landscapes, and a unique blend of action and exploration, Metroid has left a lasting impact on the gaming industry, influencing numerous titles within the adventure and action genres.
